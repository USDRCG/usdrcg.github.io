Workshops

What We Offer

If you've ever considered using the high-performance computer for your research, now is the time! We have numerous sotware packages and applications installed that are free for USD-affiliated researchers to use. We are here to get you started, to teach you the basics of using the supercomputer. You will also learn how to schedule jobs and automate redundant tasks.


  • Learn how to get connected to the HPC
  • We can help with large data transfers
  • Troubleshooting? We got this.
  • Plenty of manuals and guides for users

More Information

What to Expect We design the workshop for beginners.

We ask that you bring your laptop. We'll let you know what you need to install. Our instructors will guide you through activities and help if you get stuck.
